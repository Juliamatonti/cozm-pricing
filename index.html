Deployment Strategy

Rollout Approach

Our recommended phased, risk-based approach to deploying end-to-end compliance management for Equinor.

Our Recommendation

Phased, Risk-Based Approach

Based on our experience, we recommend a phased risk-based rollout rather than a big-bang or strictly country-by-country approach. This manages the majority of compliance risk quickly while enabling an earlier go-live date.

  • Initial upfront risk assessment using existing travel data
  • Identify 5 priority host/home country combinations
  • Phase 1 focuses on highest-risk locations
  • Phase 2 expands to remaining lower-priority countries
Priority Location Criteria

Risk Assessment Factors

Priority locations are identified by scoring each country combination across several risk dimensions to ensure effort is focused where compliance exposure is greatest.

  • Volume of trips to that country
  • Average length of trips
  • Purpose of travel
  • Host country enforcement approach
  • Potential fines and penalties
